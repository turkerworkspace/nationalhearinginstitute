2026 OTC Hearing Aid Evaluation Framework

We score consumer hearing devices across four areas. The full scoring rubric is published on our methodology page.

Evaluation Area Why It Matters What We Look For
Speech ClarityMost users want better conversations, not just louder sound.Multi-channel processing, noise reduction, feedback control.
Ease of UseSeniors often need simple setup and daily reliability.Rechargeable design, simple controls, clear instructions.
PersonalizationDifferent hearing profiles need different sound adjustment.Self-hearing test, app control, frequency tuning.
Consumer ProtectionTrial periods and warranties reduce buying risk.Home trial, strong warranty, responsive support.
